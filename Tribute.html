VIRAT KOHLI : THE KING

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the best batsman in the world.He can also called as king of Cricket..kohli has served as the captain of the Indian cricket team and led the team to significant victories.

Early Life and Career

Born on November 5, 1988, in, Delhi, India, His aggressive batting style and consistency, and leadership qualities have made him a prominent figure in international cricket. He plays right handed-batting. He is comsidered as one of the Greatest batsman of all time (G.O.A.T).

Achievements

  • Led India to its first Test series win in Australia(2018-2019).
  • Most Test wins as an Indian Captain.
  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna in 2018.
  • Second-most centuries in ODIs, behind Sachin Tendulkar.
  • Holds the record for the fastest century in ODIs by an Indian(52 balls)
